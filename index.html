Our Mission

Through art competitions hosted in Nathupur, Gurugram, and partner communities worldwide, we mentor under-resourced youth artists with supplies, coaching, and storytelling workshops. Our goal is to champion equitable art education, create youth art opportunities that transcend geography, amplify underprivileged voices, and foster appreciation for diverse cultural backgrounds across the globe.

2025 Art Prompt

Movement
