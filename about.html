About the Competition

Our Mission

Through art competitions hosted in diverse cultural settings, we aim to empower children globally through the medium of art and writing and strive to promote the arts and self-expression among the next generation of artists. Through this, we will also foster self expression, and promote the appreciation and acceptance of diverse backgrounds across the world.

Artists of Tomorrow believes that every child has a unique perspective worth sharing. Our competition provides young artists with a platform to express themselves, develop their artistic skills, and connect with a global community of peers.

By encouraging artistic expression from diverse backgrounds, we hope to foster cross-cultural understanding and appreciation, ultimately building a more connected and empathetic world through art.

Our Impact

Artistic Development

We encourage young artists to develop their skills and find their unique artistic voice.

Cultural Exchange

Our competition bridges cultural gaps by showcasing diverse perspectives through art.

Self-Expression

We create a safe space for youth to express themselves authentically through art.

Creative Confidence

We build confidence in young artists by recognizing and celebrating their work.
