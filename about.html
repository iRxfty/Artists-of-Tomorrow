About Artists of Tomorrow

Our Mission

Through art competitions hosted in diverse cultural settings, we aim to empower children globally through the medium of art and writing and strive to promote the arts and self-expression among the next generation of artists. In Nathupur, Gurugram, and beyond, we invest in underprivileged youth artists by funding supplies, mentorship, and storytelling platforms that celebrate their lived experiences and unlock youth art opportunities that would otherwise remain out of reach.

Our Vision

We believe that every child has a unique perspective and story to tell. By providing a platform for artistic expression, we aim to build an equitable global arts ecosystem where under-resourced classrooms in India and around the world gain access to mentors, exhibitions, and career pathways. In doing so, we ensure that community stories from Gurugram to the global stage shape the future of art, and this vision comes to life as we:

  • Encourage creative thinking and self-expression
  • Develop confidence and pride in personal heritage and experiences
  • Foster cross-cultural understanding and appreciation
  • Recognize and nurture artistic talent in young people
  • Create opportunities for children to have their voices heard
