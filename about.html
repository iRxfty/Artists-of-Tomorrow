About Artists of Tomorrow

Our Mission

Through art competitions hosted in diverse cultural settings, we aim to empower children globally through the medium of art and writing and strive to promote the arts and self-expression among the next generation of artists. Through this, we will also foster self expression, and promote the appreciation and acceptance of diverse backgrounds across the world.

Our Vision

We believe that every child has a unique perspective and story to tell. By providing a platform for artistic expression, we aim to:

  • Encourage creative thinking and self-expression
  • Develop confidence and pride in personal heritage and experiences
  • Foster cross-cultural understanding and appreciation
  • Recognize and nurture artistic talent in young people
  • Create opportunities for children to have their voices heard

Our Judges

Our competition features a distinguished panel of judges who are committed to supporting young artists and recognizing their talent.
