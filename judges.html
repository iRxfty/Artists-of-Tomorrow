Judging Process

Our judging process is designed to be fair, transparent, and focused on recognizing artistic merit and self-expression. Each artwork is evaluated based on our comprehensive rubric that considers:

  • Adherence to the prompt
  • Originality
  • Self-expression
  • Technical skills
  • Writing supplement

For complete details about our judging process and rubric, please visit our Rubric page.
