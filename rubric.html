Judging Rubric

Competition Rubric

All submissions will be evaluated using the following criteria. Each category is scored on a scale of 1-5 points:

  • 5 points: Student exceeds expectations
  • 4 points: Student meets expectations
  • 3 points: Student approaches expectations
  • 2 points: Student touches upon expectations
  • 1 point: Student does not meet any expectations
Criteria Description Points
Adherence to the Prompt While we aim to nurture self-expression, responses must incorporate the themes of the prompt. Any submissions that we feel do not adhere to the prompt will be eliminated. 5
Originality Student illustrates their experience originally, showing little to no outside influence or reused ideas. 5
Self-Expression Student utilizes unique and distinct methods to express themselves within aspects of their piece. 10 (2x)
Use of Skills Student effectively demonstrates utilization of art skills, elements (line, shape, form, space, texture, value, and colors), and effort in their drawings. 5
Writing The student's writing supplement provided by the student explains their artwork and aspects of their distinct expression within their piece. 5
Total Possible Points 30
