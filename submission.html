Submit Your Art

Submission Process

1

Create Your Artwork

Select one of the four prompts and create your artwork on the paper provided by your school. Remember to use only pencil as specified in the rules.

2

Complete Your Writing Supplement

Write your supplement (maximum 250 words) on the back of your artwork paper. You may write in Hindi or English.

3

Register Online

Fill out the form below to receive your unique submission number.

4

Label Your Artwork

Write your submission number, name, date, and grade level on the back of your artwork.

5

Scan and Upload

Scan both sides of your artwork and upload the files using the form below.

6

Submit Physical Artwork

Give your physical artwork to your school representative, who will mail it to our judging panel.
