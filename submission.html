Submit Your Artwork

Submission Process

01

Create Your Artwork

Choose one of the four prompts and create your artwork using only the paper and pencil provided by your school.

02

Write Your Supplement

On the back of your artwork, write a supplement (max 250 words) in Hindi or English that explains your piece.

03

Complete Form Below

Fill out the submission form below to receive your unique identification number.

04

Mark Your Artwork

Write your identification number, name, date, and grade level on the back of your artwork.

05

Scan & Upload

Scan both sides of your artwork and upload the files through the form below.

06

Submit Physical Copy

Submit your physical artwork to your school coordinator who will mail it to our judges.
