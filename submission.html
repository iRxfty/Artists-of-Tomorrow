Submission Process

1

Create Your Artwork

Create your artwork based on the "Movement" prompt using the paper provided by your school. Remember to use only pencil as specified in the rules.

2

Complete Your Writing Supplement

Write your supplement (maximum 250 words) on the back of your artwork paper. You may write in Hindi or English.

3

Register Online

Fill out the registration form to receive your unique submission number.

4

Label Your Artwork

Write your submission number, name, date, and grade level on the back of your artwork.

5

Scan and Upload

Scan both sides of your artwork and upload the files as instructed in the form.

6

Submit Physical Artwork

Give your physical artwork to your school representative, who will mail it to our judging panel.
